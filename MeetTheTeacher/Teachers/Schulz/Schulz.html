Why I Love Teaching CTE:

The number one reason I love teaching at SICTC
is because of the students. If I can help a student realize that they can be
hired in to a quality career, be accepted to a post secondary school, or just have
some skills and confidence to succeed in life, I see the students come in the first week
or two, wanting to try something different they haven't tried before, or following up on a
claim that they could learn with their hands here at SICTC. or even just wanting to get out
of their home school and I try to turn them into excited, confident, skillful employees that
employers around our area are starving for. That is why I love teaching at SICTC. I have the opportunity to change lives.
