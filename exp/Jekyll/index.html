Advanced Manufacturing Technology

Advanced Manufacturing and Industrial Maintenance introduces students to manufacturing processes and the design, operation, and maintenance of modern production systems. Topics include safety, measurement, machining, electrical circuits, PLCs, mechanical, pneumatic, and hydraulic systems, plus automation and robotics. Learning combines classroom instruction, lab work, and self-directed projects for hands-on experience.

Architecture & Engineering Technology

Architecture & Engineering Design Technology prepares students for university programs in architecture, engineering, interior design, and construction management. Students study design concepts for residential and commercial structures, mechanical design, CAD software (AutoCAD, Revit, SolidWorks), construction methods, surveying, and CNC basics. The first year explores both architecture and engineering, while the second year allows specialization in architectural design, mechanical design, or digital electronics.
